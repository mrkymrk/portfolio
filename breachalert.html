BreachAlert

If your data is posted on the Dark Web the BreachAlert application will find your data anywhere and will instantly alert you.

_01the challenge

Design a solution that quickly alerts those involved in the processing of information at an organisation to any breaches of their data, increasing their confidence in the security of their information, and potentially avoiding huge fines under GDPR regulations.

_02my role

For this project I lead the whole UX process including:

  • Developing personas
  • Wireframes
  • UI design
  • HTML & CSS build
  • Analysis
  • Iterating improvements

_03outcomes

Since its initial creation in 2016 BreachAlert has saw a quick uptake amongst different sized organisations in various areas including communications, healthcare and insurance - often being the tool of choice due to its ease of use for users with varying degress of technical ability.
