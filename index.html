process

Although every projects offers individual challenges, sticking to some fundamental processes from the beginning is the best way to maximise success.

_01discover

Discovering all we can about the people we are designing for and defining the problems we intend on solving for them is the key to beginning a succesful project.

_02design

After we know all we can about who we're designing for we can begin designing the structure, navigation and user interface elements.

_03deliver

When we feel we have found the ideal design solution, its then time to build and deliver the experience to the user then watch learn from their use.

_04measure

Once delivered, its time to monitor, measure and analyse how the delivered solution met the needs of the user and business and continue to refine based on our findings.
