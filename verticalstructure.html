Vertical Structure

Updating and improving the overall online experience across all devices for Vertical Structure's current and potential clients.

_01the challenge

The challenge posed from Vertical Structure was that their previous website felt outdated and having been designed in the early stages of the company, their range of services expanded but this had not reflected on their website, therefore they needed a website redesign making their availabe services clearer to both current and potential clients.

_02my role

My role updating Vertical Structures web presence included:

  • Competitor analysis
  • IA Design
  • Wireframes
  • UI design
  • HTML & CSS build

_03outcomes

Shortly after the delivery of their new website Vertical Structure found more enquiries for their previously lesser known services and much more feedback from attendees of their courses due to the new easier method to submit feedback on their site.
